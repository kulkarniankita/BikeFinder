Bike Finder

AN SMS SERVICE FOR BIKE SHARE TORONTO


Bike Finder is an SMS service designed for Bike Share Toronto which delivers real time information about station locations and bike availability.

Text an intersection to 647-559-0509 to receive up-to-the-minute results for bicycle and dock availability at your nearest
Bike Share station



Service Design

Bike Finder was designed as an answer to data accessibility and equal opportunity needs in Bike Share's operations.



Focus

The creators believe that Bike Share information should be available to riders who do not own a smartphone or choose not to use the existing app.



Costs

Bike Finder was created as a low-cost tool to quickly and easily help riders locate their nearest station and determine the availability of bicycles and docks.
